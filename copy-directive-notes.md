# Copy Directive Key Points

## ABSOLUTE BANS
- "credit repair" in any form
- "building business credit" / net-30 / credit stacking / trade lines
- "Strategic Data Furnishing" as mechanism/pillar
- 3-pillar framework (must use 4-step)
- Client does the work framing
- Vague "hidden factors" without naming 27 secondary bureaus

## REQUIRED ELEMENTS
- "27 secondary data furnishers/bureaus" + name 2-3 (Innovis, LexisNexis, SageStream)
- Legal mechanism: cease & desist + FCRA/FTC/CFPB/OCC
- "99% of the time they can't prove it"
- Domino/cascade effect to Big 3
- 4-step process: Audit → Attack → Optimize → Submit
- Done-for-you + no results no fee
- Case studies with specific numbers
- $50K-$250K in 14-30 days

## 4-STEP PROCESS
1. 30-Bureau Deep Audit
2. Secondary Bureau Attack
3. Funding Profile Optimization
4. Insider Lender Submission

## 3 APPROVED CASE STUDIES ONLY
1. Restaurant Owner: $45K/mo revenue, 6 rejections, $150K approved in 1 week
2. Clark: 520 score → $75K 0% APR in 32 days
3. Tom: $110K funding → $1M sales in <1 year
4. Aggregate: 65 owners, $6M total funding last year

## TONE
- Investigative, urgent, slightly conspiratorial
- Short punchy paragraphs
- Concrete specifics always
- 27 bureaus = VILLAIN, client = VICTIM, us = INSIDER
- REMOVAL not ADDITION framing

## VSL STRUCTURE (18-22 min)
Hook → Credibility → Problem Revealed → Why Alternatives Fail → Mechanism → Process → Social Proof → Cost of Inaction → Offer → Risk Reversal → Scarcity + Close

## 5 AD ANGLES
1. Problem Awareness (unexplained denials)
2. Enemy/Us vs Them (system rigged)
3. Case Study/Proof-First
4. Contrarian/Belief Shift
5. Urgency/Opportunity Window
