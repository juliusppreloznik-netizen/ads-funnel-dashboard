# Pattern Analysis — Phase 2

After extracting from all transcripts in the session, analyze across all of them and identify:

- Recurring pain themes: which pains show up across 50%+ of transcripts
- Most emotionally charged pain: which pain consistently gets the strongest emotional response
- Dominant obstacle pattern: what prospects most commonly BELIEVE is blocking them
- Most common failed solution: what they've tried most often that didn't work
- Strongest buying signal pattern: what moments or language most reliably predict a close
- The Gap: the distance between where they are and where they want to be, in their own words
- Cost of Inaction language: how they describe what happens if nothing changes — this is the single most important copy ammunition in the entire extraction
